Khaana was created to help busy individuals and families enjoy healthy meals without the hassle of cooking every day.

Instead of single takeout orders, the founders built a service that delivers full meal plans with multiple meals at once.

The idea came from the founders' own struggles with long workdays and inconsistent eating habits.

Their solution combined nutrition, convenience, and variety into one simple offering.

What started as a small pilot program quickly grew into a trusted service for customers seeking control over their diets.

From the beginning, Khaana's mission has been to make meal planning stress-free with affordable, delicious, and ready-to-eat meals.

Customers could pick plans designed for fitness, families, or dietary preferences and receive several days of meals in one delivery.

Over time, Khaana added seasonal menus, family packages, and options for vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free lifestyles.

With locally sourced ingredients, chef-crafted recipes, and reliable delivery systems, the service became known for quality and convenience.

Today, Khaana continues to grow while staying committed to innovation, sustainability, and making healthy eating simple for everyone.
